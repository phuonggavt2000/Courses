Douglas Schatz

Animation & Video Artist

Hello! I'm a motion graphics artist and video editor specializing in short video loops and animated GIFs with a focus on social media. I'm fascinated by loops and digital graphics and nice colors. I've created work for Cosmopolitan, Esquire, Popular Mechanics, and I've had works shown at The New Museum, Eyebeam, and Red Bull Studios. On the side, I do a little bit of web design and web development (for example: the page you're on, right now!)

I currently live in Burlington, Vermont, but I do work all around the world! If you'd like to work with me, or just say hello, send me an e-mail
